> 守听询问正确表达：**Is this frequency in use? / Is anyone at this frequency?**，❌排除**Calling you, Roger?**

| 表述 | 评价 |
|-----|------|
| "Is this frequency in use?" | ✓标准 |
| "Is anyone at this frequency?" | ✓标准 |
| "Anybody here?" | ✓简洁可接受 |
| "Calling you, Roger?" | ✗非规范 |

**错误原因**：A项"Calling you"是主动呼叫，"Roger?"是确认回复，不是询问频率占用。标准问法用**"Is"开头疑问句**。
